Student Login Page

A number of classroom interfaces have a common design flaw: the student login is not user-friendly for young students. For young children, logging in includes too many steps and too many pieces of information to remember, and involves too many typing/navigation skills. In my design, students find their name in the dropdown and instead of having to enter a letter/numerical password, the user's password is made up of a number of pictures that they have to put in order. Supportive features could be added to assist users with remembering the pictures and the order of the pictures if they enter their password incorrectly.
